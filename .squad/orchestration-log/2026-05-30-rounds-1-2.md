## Outcomes

### Security Hardening
- ✅ **Timing side-channel eliminated** (#173): Constant-time token comparison prevents cryptanalysis
- ✅ **GitHub Actions supply-chain secured** (#155): 13 actions pinned to immutable SHAs instead of mutable tags
- ✅ **Docker image pinning** (#162): 26 FROM lines pinned by digest for reproducible, attestable builds

### Correctness & Reliability
- ✅ **HA integration robustness** (#149): Clear local error instead of opaque server-side `auth_invalid`
- ✅ **Telemetry race condition fixed** (#182): Pipeline timing snapshots now stable across scheduling jitter
- ✅ **mDNS collision eliminated** (#183): Wyoming service identity unified; per-host unique names by default
- ✅ **Agent registration validation** (#176): malformed URIs now return 400 (not 500)
